Rahm Kohlrabi

Description

Creamy side dish. Best with meatloaf or meatballs.

Ingredients

  • 3 x turnip cabbage
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • 2 teaspoons vegetable broth, instant
  • 150 ml sweet cream
  • Salt and pepper, white from the mill
  • Nutmeg, freshly grated

Steps

  1. Peel the turnip cabbage and cut into sticks

  2. Boil in lightly salted water for about 20 minutes

  3. Drain and reserve 250 ml of the cooking water

  4. Fry the turnip lightly in the butter and dust the flour over it

  5. Sweat lightly and deglaze with cooking water and cream

  6. Sir in the vegetable stock and let the sauce simmer for 2 minutes

  7. Season with salt, white pepper and nutmeg
