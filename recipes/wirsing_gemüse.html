Wirsing Gemüse

Description

Hearty side dish, will melt your hearth.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg Savoy,cleaned, finely chopped
  • 1 chopped Onion
  • 100g Beacon
  • 1 tsp. butter
  • 75 ml milk
  • 250 ml broth
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • 100ml cream
  • salt and pepper
  • nutmeg

Steps

  1. Lightly brown the onion, finely chopped bacon and butter in a saucepan over medium heat.

  2. Add the chopped savoy cabbage, fill up with the hot meat broth and let everything steam until soft over a moderate heat.

  3. Mix the flour with the milk until smooth and add to the cooked cabbage.

  4. Boil briefly. Season to taste with pepper, salt and nutmeg.

  5. Finally, fold the cream under the finished, no longer boiling vegetables.

  6. Serve with boiled potatoes and coarse bratwurst or other pan-fried meat.
