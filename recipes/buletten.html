Buletten

Description

Meat meat meat.

Ingredients

  • 1 bun
  • 1 onion
  • 1 egg
  • 500g minced meat, mixed
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • 1 tsp strained marjoram, dried
  • 1 tsp paprika powder
  • lots of pepper, from the mill
  • 2 tsp parsley, dried

Steps

  1. Soak buns in water

  2. Peel the onion and cut into fine cubes

  3. Add the egg, onions and seasonings to the ground beef and mix very wll, either with a large spoon or with your hands. Do not work with the mixer, the meatballs often become tough!

  4. Squeeze out the bun mass very well, either with your hands or between two boards, add to the mince and mix well again.

  5. Form even dumplings and flatten the surface

  6. Heat a heaby frying pan with oil and put in the dumplings, sear briefly on both sides and then finish frying for about 15 -20 minutes (turning carefully 1 - 2 times) over medium / low heat.
